F = Fair, OV-1 = Overcast 1, OV-2 = Overcast 2, ST = Storm

  1. Roll one die at start of each turn, referring to the proper column on the WEATHER TABLE. Indicate current weather on Weather Record Track.
  2. Modify weather die roll as indicated on the Weather Modifier Chart.
  3. If weather is STORM (ST), place weather marker on the "Storm 1" box on the Record Track during initial storm turn. Weather will AUTOMATICALLY be STORM-2 following the STORM-1 turn. On the thrid turn, the die roll is modified by adding 2. Once two turns of STORM have elapsed, there are only two possibilities for any subsequent weather. If a STORM result is rolled, the weather is STORM-2 again, and you add 2 to the next turn's die roll. If any result othe than STORM is rolled, the weather is automatically FAIR. NOTE: after six consecutive turns of STORM, the weather automatically becomes FAIR without the die being rolled.

Weather Effects Chart

FAIR No Effect.
OVERCAST-1
  • No air operations except rail interdiction, strategic interdiction, and air supply.
OVERCAST-2
  • No air operations except strategic interdiction.
  • Subtract 1 from Naval Bombardment Table die rolls.
  • Beach Landing Capacity HALVED (round down). Ports and Mulberries normal.
STORM-1 and -2
  • No air operations of any type.
  • No Naval Bombardment.
  • Beach Landing Boxes may not be used. Ports and Mulberries normal.
