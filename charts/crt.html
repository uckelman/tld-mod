NOTES:

  1. Odds less than 1-2 = automatic attacker elimination.
  2. Defending units add 1 to the die roll for each disrupted marker.
  3. All die roll modifications are cumulative, but may not be less than −1 or more than 8

KEY:

  1. ATTACKER
    DEFENDER
  2. LS = Lose one single step from any one of the attacking or defending units, as applicable.
  3. L1 = Lose one step from each battalion-sized (or larger) unit involved in this combat. Must, however, lose at least ONE step, even if from a company-sized unit.
  4. L2 = Lose two steps from each battalion-sized (or larger) unit involved in this combat.
  5. A2,3 = All units may advance, at player's option, the number of hexes indicated, in any direction.
  6. R2,3 = All units must retreat the number of hexes indicated. Owning player retreats each unit individually. First priority of retreat route must be into hexes that are not adjacent to enemy units where choice is available. Units that cannot retreat full amount of hexes due to terrain restrictions or presence of enemy units are eliminated instead.
  7. E = Automatic elimination of ALL STEPS of ALL UNITS involved in this combat.
  8. — = No effect, no losses, no advance or retreat.
