FORTIFICATIONS CHART

TYPE UNIT COUNTER CAPACITY ZONE OF CONTROL SPECIAL EFFECTS/REMARKS
FORTIFIED AREA two units of company, battalion size, or HQ or supply or any combination six adjacent hexes
  1. Counters must be placed in hexes containing fortified area symbol of the appropriate name.
  2. Multiplies totalled defense strength of occupying units by two or three times, as stipulated.
  3. Defending units not halved when unsupplied.
MINOR FORTS one unit: company, battalion, HQ, or supply six adjacent hexes
  1. counters must be placed in hexes containing minor forts symbol of the appropriate name.
  2. Doubles defense strength of occupying unit.
  3. Defending units not halved when unsupplied.
COASTAL STRONGPOINTS NONE: defends with intrinsic defense strength only Extends into front three hexes ONLY ("Front" is top of counter)
  1. Counters must be aligned as printed on mapboard, in hexes containing coastal strongpoint symbol of the appropriate value.
  2. LS result during amphibious assault allows attacker to advance into hex.
  3. Intrinsic defense strength not halved when unsupplied.
LANDFRONTE FORTIFICATIONS three units of company, battalion, HQ, supply, or any combination within stacking limit Extends into front three hexes ONLY ("Front" is top of counter)
  1. Counters must be aligned as printed on Cherbourg diagram.
  2. Counters, like coastal strongpoints, have intrinsic defense strength and must be treated as a regular combat unit for combat resolution purposes.
  3. Intrinsic defense strength not halved when unsupplied.
FIELD FORTIFICATIONS three units of company, battalion, HQ, or supply or any combination within stacking limitation Extends into front three hexes ONLY ("Front" is top of counter)
  1. Once placed on mapboard, alignment may not change.
  2. May only be built on TOWN hexes as per rules.
  3. Does not affect occupying units' defense strength.
  4. Defending units halved when unsupplied.

KEY:

  1. CAPACITY: Number of unit counters able to benefit from fortification at the same time.
  2. DEFENSE STRENGTH MODIFIER: Units under fortification modify defense strength: 'x2' or 'x3': Defense strength of units multiplied by two or three. '2' or '6': Represents intrinsic defense strenght of fortification unit itself. '0': Defense strength of units not modified: remains normal.
  3. BOMBARDMENT MODIFIER: Modify results of naval and air bombardment die roll by subtracting 1 or 2.

NOTES:

  1. Coastal Strongpoints and Landfronte Fortifications, when defending only, can absorb losses like regular combat units. Each has one step.
  2. Coastal Strongpoints and Landfronte Fortifications, and units DEFENDING under Fortified Aeas and Minor Forts are not halved when unsupplied.
  3. All fortifications lose their zone of control when disrupted.
  4. For Landfronte Fortifications, add intrinsic defense strength to the defense strength of the units under Landfronte counter.
